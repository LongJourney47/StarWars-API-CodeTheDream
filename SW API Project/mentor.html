General Information

      Master Obi-wan Kenobi is a promiment figure during the Clone Wars era of Galactic History. During this period he was involved in many battles against the Seperatists and was master of a renowned jedi named Anakin Skywalker. He would later still play a important role in Galatic History as the mentor to one of Anakin's children, Luke Skywalker. Both of whom in their own ways contributed to the war effort of the Rebel Alliance against imperial control during the Imperial era in episode 4, "A New Hope".
